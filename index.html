    • Scaled Jump in Gravity-reduced Virtual Environments

    MyoungGon Kim, Sunglk Cho, Tanh Quang Tran, Seong-Pil Kim, Ohung Kwon, and JungHyun Han.
    IEEE transactions on visualization and computer graphics 23.4 (2017): 1360-1368.



  • Jump motion is up or down scaled in virtual environments
  • Extent to which a jump can be scaled without the discrepancy being noticed by the user is measured
  • Based on the tolerable range of scaling, retargeted jump is developed
  • In the retargeted jump, a user can jump onto virtaul objects while jumping on the real-world flat floor
